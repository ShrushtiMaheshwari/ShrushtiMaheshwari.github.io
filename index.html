Hi, I am Shreyas. I am a machine learning enthusiast with a BE in Computer Science from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore and an MS in Computer Science (Machine Learning Track) from Columbia University.

I am currently working as an engineer in the Perception team at JDX, the self-driving department of JD.com. I build real-time memory efficient models for LiDAR based object detection using neural networks.

I interned the summer of 2019 at Telenav, a car navigation company based in Santa Clara, as a Data Analyst Intern, from June 2018 to August 2018. During my internship, I was assigned the task of implementing a software that will help to visualize users' trips, generate insights about those trips and also identify any bugs or issues that might be present in the logs received from the trip. I used tools like graphviz and networkx to generate my visualizations and analyse the resulting graphs.

I have previously interned at Paypal Inc., Singapore as a Software Engineering Intern, from January 2016 to May 2016. During my internship, I created automated suites for continuous testing using Nemo, an open source library developed by Paypal, for NodeJS. I used Jenkins server to run these automated tests.

I am an out-and-out techie and I am passionate about competing in hackathons and other kinds of programming competitions. Here is a list of the competitions I have competed in the past few years:

  • HackPrinceton (November, 2017)
  • Google Code Jam (August, 2016)
  • iNTUition, a 24 hour hackathon organized by the Open Source Society in NTU (March, 2016)
  • ACM ICPC Programming Competition in Kuala Lumpur (November, 2014)

After multiple tries, I finally managed to win the "Best Implementation" for implementing a Raffle on the Nebulas blockchain for the Silicon Valley Blockchain Developer Hackathon in 2018!

I am also passionate about playing chess, solving the Rubik's cube and reading books.
